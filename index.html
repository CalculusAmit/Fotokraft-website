Fotokraft is a Creative Direction
Photography Club

It was started by a group of students of Jorhat Engineering College in the year 2012 on the 18th of April FotoKraft is responsible for documenting each and every activity in the college premises, be it formal functions organized by the administration or informal activities that include student participation. By far it has been the most active Club of Jorhat Engineering College as it happens to be an inexplicable part of the socio-cultural structure of the college by itself. Besides that the other activities of the clubs include photo-shootouts, holding photography- workshops, competitions, seminars and photo exhibitions. The club has its own photo gallery in the college premises which displays the immense talent of its members

What we do -

Fotokraft engages in various photography-related activities to foster creativity and document college life through the lens.

Photo Shootouts

Regular photography sessions and outdoor shoots to capture stunning visuals and improve technical skills of club members.

Photography Workshops

Comprehensive workshops covering camera techniques, composition, lighting, and post-processing to enhance photography skills.

Exhibitions & Competitions

Organizing photography exhibitions, competitions like Creatrix, and showcasing member talents through our campus gallery.
